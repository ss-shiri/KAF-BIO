Scope

This is an aggregator, not a verifier. Listing a source is not an endorsement of it. Entries carry the publisher's own headline or title and a link to the original. Nothing is hosted here, nothing is rewritten, nothing is scored. Preprints, retracted papers and low credibility outlets are shown deliberately, each marked with its type so you can judge for yourself. Verification and assessment happen downstream, not on this page.
